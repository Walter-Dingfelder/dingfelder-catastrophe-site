How it works

How C.A.T.A.S.T.R.O.P.H.E. reaches your community

C.A.T.A.S.T.R.O.P.H.E. is designed to protect people during high-consequence events, but it does not appear in a vacuum. It is part of the A.I.R.O.N. deployment ecosystem, which means local business participation, responder awareness, and community interest all help determine where this protection can grow next.

1

A.I.R.O.N. is deployed in a local business or operation

A.I.R.O.N. provides the local operational foundation: industrial intelligence, situational awareness, system continuity, and structured response logic inside participating facilities and operations.

2

C.A.T.A.S.T.R.O.P.H.E. becomes the emergency-response and human-guidance layer

When severe conditions unfold, C.A.T.A.S.T.R.O.P.H.E. supports safer human action through guided response structure, clearer priorities, and better continuity when confusion, disruption, or loss of communication begins to take hold.

3

Local businesses, responders, and communities gain a stronger path through crisis

Businesses gain better continuity and readiness. Emergency responders gain clearer local support structure. Communities gain a stronger safety network tied to the places where risk already exists.
